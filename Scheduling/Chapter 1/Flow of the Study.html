Scheduling System of Mandaue City


Flow of the Study

The Scheduler aims to provide a more accurate way of scheduling. It will help the deans or the department to plot a class schedules and faculty load efficientlyand to manage student’s information.

InputObservation Interview ➨ Process Waterfall Model ➨ Output Scheduling System

Input

The researchers observed the existing problem of the school regarding the manual scheduling and conduct interview in gathering data. The gathered data will be served as input to the flow of the study.

Process

The researchers used the waterfall model. The researchers conduct a meeting for the proposed system. After this, they conducted an interview from Mandaue City College for the further details of the proposed system. They gathered more information to complement the documents of the system.

Output

After gathering of data, the researchers analyze the gathered data and proposed a scheduling system that could help the department to manage student’s information and to provide them a more accurate and efficient way of scheduling.
