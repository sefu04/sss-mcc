Scheduling System of Mandaue City College


Project Context

The process of the scheduling system of Mandaue City College are the following:

    1.The schedule and the units/ hours are based on the prospectus.
    2.The Deans/Department chair will plot the Major subject first.
    3.The Deans/Department chair will plot the Minor subject after.
    4.Students Assistant check if there are subject conflict in terms of time/room/instructor.
    5.Assigning of Instructor
    6.Department chair send the finalize list of subject schedule to the registrar for Enrolment.
    7.Registrar then encode the finalize list of subjects.

To come up schedule, students need to complete the following requirements:

    1.SchoolID
    2.Clearance
    3.Class Card
    4.Previews Class Card
    5.Previews Study load
    6.Prospectus
    7.Enrollment Form
