Scheduling System of Mandaue City College


SCOPE & LIMITATION OF THE STUDY

Scope
This system is design for class scheduling in Mandaue City College. The proponents aim to develop a system that will provide a more accurate and effective way of scheduling. This covers the subject codes, subject name, time and day scheduled and the room, and number of units.

Limitations
This system will only be intended for Mandaue City College. It will not include the accounting transactions. Accounting and enrolment process will not be a responsibility for the system. It can only be for scheduling purposes. It can only shows the schedule of the subjects.
