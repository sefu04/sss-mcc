Scheduling System of Mandaue City College

Objectives of the Study

The main objective of the study is to assess the class scheduling system of Mandaue City College. These are the specific objectives the proponents want to achieve:

• Create a system that provides a more accurate way of scheduling of classes.

• To provide a system that displays schedule information and effective information.

• Design a system that eliminates the use of manual scheduling and provide easier and effective way of scheduling.

• Develop a system that can make an effective and efficient scheduling

• Develop a system that could help the deans and department staffs to immediate change course schedule if there are changes occur.
